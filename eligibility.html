Eligibility Requirements

Basic Requirements

  • Age between 18 and 65 years
  • Minimum weight of 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Good general health
  • Normal blood pressure
  • Hemoglobin level at least 12.5 g/dL

Health Conditions

  • No cold, flu, or infection symptoms
  • No chronic illnesses like heart disease, kidney disease, etc.
  • No history of hepatitis B or C
  • No HIV/AIDS
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding

Time-Based Restrictions

  • Wait 3 months after getting a tattoo or piercing
  • Wait 6 months after major surgery
  • Wait 12 months after getting a blood transfusion
  • Wait 4 weeks after travel to malaria-endemic areas
  • Wait 3 days after dental procedures

Medication & Vaccinations

  • Not taking antibiotics for active infection
  • No recent vaccinations (varies by type)
  • Not on blood thinners like Warfarin
  • No recent use of recreational drugs
  • Not undergoing cancer treatment
